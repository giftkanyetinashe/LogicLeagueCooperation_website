Organization Profile // LLC

The Strategic League of Intelligence.

Logic League Cooperation is an umbrella organization designed to act as the External Brain for Managing Directors and CEOs. We are not a traditional IT firm; we are a multidisciplinary league of Data Scientists, Systems Architects, and Brand Strategists.

What is the Cooperation?

The "Cooperation" represents our ecosystem approach. Every project we take on is touched by our four specialized arms. We believe that a high-performance system (Digital Lab) is useless without the data strategy to guide it (Insight), and a brilliant strategy is ineffective if it cannot be communicated with authority (Designs).
